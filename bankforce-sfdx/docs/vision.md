## 1. Business Context
BankForce CRM is a Salesforce-based solution designed to support a mid-size bank in managing customer relationships, service requests, and digital service channels.

The platform focuses on improving customer experience, operational efficiency, and scalability through automation and omnichannel service.

---

## 2. Business Objectives
- Centralize customer information in a single CRM platform
- Enable omnichannel customer service (bot + human agents)
- Improve SLA compliance and service quality
- Support digital transformation initiatives
- Provide scalable integration with core banking systems

---

## 3. Stakeholders
- Customer Service Agents
- Operations Managers
- IT / Integration Team
- End Customers

---

## 4. Assumptions
- Salesforce Service Cloud is the main service platform
- External banking systems will be integrated via APIs
- English is the technical language used in the solution
